Burmese Chicken And Potatoes Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. of boneless chicken thighs, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 4 red potatoes, peeled and cut into quarters or 10-15 baby potatoes
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp. grated ginger
  • 5 tbsp. vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp. paprika
  • Â½ tsp. ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp. ground cumin
  • salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with grated ginger, minced garlic, turmeric powder, paprika, and salt for at least 2 hours.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil and fry the onions till caramelized and fragrant.
  3. Add the marinated chicken to the pot and sear for 10 minutes.
  4. Add 1 cup of water and bring it to a boil.
  5. Then, reduce the heat to medium and cook for 15 minutes.
  6. Add the potatoes and cook till tender. Adjust water as necessary.
  7. Cover the pot for a few minutes.
  8. Ready to serve.
